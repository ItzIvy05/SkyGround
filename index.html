A roleplaying experience unlike any other

SkyGround is a free, auto-installable modpack for Skyrim that builds on Skyrim’s foundation to deliver an immersive fantasy roleplaying game like no other.

With over 2500+ mods and deep integration, SkyGround creates a living world where you become who you want, act as you want, and thrive in a deeply simulated universe.

Explore a reactive world

The world of Wildlander is a complex web of simulation, where every choice reverberates out into a new story. Encounter inspired adventures wandering the roads. Trade in localized economies with real market forces. Make friends & enemies, and earn your reputation. Interact with factions dynamically pursuing their goals.

Survive the frigid wastes

Wildlander evokes the harsh cold north, a realistic depiction of life in a harsh, frozen homeland. Death is common when venturing beyond the city walls, where only the strong and clever survive. Combat is visceral and unforgiving, and one misstep can mark the difference between life and death.

Be whoever you want

Earn a living as a humble carpenter or a courtly noble. Strike out into the tundra and hunt for survival and profit. Hire mercenaries and travel the roads as a merchant. Plumb Skyrim’s depths as a collector of treasures. Shape your destiny however you choose.

Unleash Skyrim’s potential

Wildlander elevates the acclaimed Skyrim experience to even greater heights. Quests feature more depth, voices and alternate endings. Factions contain more content, moral dilemmas, and strict requirements to join and advance. Every part of the world is denser and more rewarding to explore.
